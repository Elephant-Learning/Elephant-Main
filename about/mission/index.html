Our Mission

We here at Elephant strive to provide access to high-quality, engaging educational services to ultimately eradicate obstacles students face in their educational journey.

Empowering every student through accessible education is worth fighting for. Elephant, an online suite of unique educational services, embodies this belief. Through Elephant, we are committed to dismantle educational barriers, especially for neurodiverse students, offering free, high-quality tools, ensuring everyone can reach their full intellectual potential.

Core Values

Fundamental Principles of Elephant

Our team at Elephant have carefully chosen our core values when developing our products in accordance with our mission. We aim to empower students to take control of their learning and achieve their full potential. These principles serve as the foundation for the actions and decisions of the organization, and help to ensure that all actions align with the company's overall vision and purpose of providing necessary advanced educational products to students everywhere

Accessibility

Making advanced educational products available and accessible to students everywhere, regardless of their location or background.

Quality

Providing high-quality, engaging and accurate educational products that meet the needs of advanced learners.

Inclusivity

Creating an inclusive and welcoming learning environment that respects and celebrates the diversity of students.

Innovation

Continuously innovating and improving our products to ensure they are up-to-date, effective and relevant to students' needs.

Support

Providing support to students and educators to help them make the most of our products and achieve their goals.

Impact

Continuously measure and track the impact of our products on students learning and progress.

Collaboration

Collaborating with educators, experts and other organizations to create better educational products and services
